Main Types

  1. Trees:
    Ashoka
  2. Shrubs: Medium-sized plants (e.g., Rose)
  3. Herbs: Small plants with soft stems (e.g., Mint)
  4. Climbers: Plants that need support (e.g., Grapevine)
  5. Creepers: Plants that spread on ground (e.g., Pumpkin)

*Different plants serve different purposes*
